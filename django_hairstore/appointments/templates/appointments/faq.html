Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of hair services including cuts, color, styling, wig installation, wig maintenance, and custom wig creation.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online through our booking system. Simply create an account, select your desired service, choose a date and time, and confirm your booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a cancellation fee.

Do you accept walk-ins?

While we prefer scheduled appointments, we do accept walk-ins based on availability. For the best service experience, we recommend booking in advance.
